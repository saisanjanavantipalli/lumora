WAVES OF TIME

A quiet walk along the shores of memory

There is a profound stillness in watching an older couple walk side-by-side against a timeless horizon. The vast, rhythmic motion of the ocean serves as a backdrop to a quiet, lifelong partnership.
Captured with a film-strip overlay, the moment transitions from a simple candid snapshot into a vivid memory frozen in time, blending the black-and-white permanence of the sea with the warm colors of a shared journey.
As the tide pulls back and pushes forward, it reminds us of the steady passage of time. Walking on the shore isn't just about the destination; it’s about the quiet rhythm of stepping in unison, leaving behind a brief trail of footprints that the water eventually claims, leaving only the memory intact.
